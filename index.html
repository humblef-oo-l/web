About me

I have some experience in optimization and scheduling problems. My current focus includes the explainability of AI, AI policy, and AI ethics. I am intrigued by the wisdom of the crowd and the challenges of mimicking human understanding and decision algorithms in machines. I'm particularly interested in exploring the decidability aspects of this paradigm. If you have thoughts to share or find anything interesting regarding these topics, feel free to shoot me an email!
